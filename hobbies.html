Rohan's Hobbies

I have been folding Origami for over 7 years now. After learning Origami through books, I have recently started to create my own Origami fold patterns using tutorials from the MIT Origami Math course.

Lego

Since the age of 5 I have been creating creations using Lego bricks. In the last few years my lego creations have become more sophisticated, including more detail and using a lot more bricks.
